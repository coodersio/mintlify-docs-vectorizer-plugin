## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Vectorized (AI Vectorizer) - Image Tracer ("we", "our", or "the Plugin") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Figma plugin service.

<Note>
  **TL;DR**: We don't store your design files or images. We only keep your Figma user ID and credit balance. Images are processed via Vectorizer.ai and immediately deleted.
</Note>

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. User Information

**What we collect**:
- Figma User ID (automatically provided by Figma)
- Figma username (for display purposes)
- Email address (for purchase receipts only, via PayPro)

**How we collect it**:
- Automatically when you install and run the plugin
- Via Figma's API
- Through PayPro payment processor (for purchases)

**Why we collect it**:
- To identify your account
- To manage your credit balance
- To send purchase confirmations
- To provide customer support

### 2. Image Data

**What we collect**:
- Images you choose to vectorize (temporary)
- Only when you click "Preview" or "Convert"

**What happens to your images**:
1. Image is uploaded to our secure server
2. Sent to Vectorizer.ai API for processing
3. Result (SVG) is sent back to you
4. **Image is immediately deleted** from our servers (within 60 seconds)
5. Vectorizer.ai also deletes the image after processing (per their policy)

**What we DON'T collect**:
- ❌ Your Figma design files
- ❌ Images you don't explicitly convert
- ❌ Any designs, components, or other Figma content
- ❌ Image metadata, EXIF data, or file history

<Warning>
  **Important**: We never store your images or design files. All image data is deleted immediately after processing. We cannot retrieve your images after conversion.
</Warning>

### 3. Usage Analytics

**What we collect** (anonymized):
- Number of conversions per user
- Preset usage statistics (which presets are popular)
- Error rates and types
- Processing times

**What we DON'T track**:
- Individual image content
- Specific files or projects
- User behavior outside the plugin
- Browsing history

**Why we collect it**:
- To improve plugin performance
- To optimize default settings
- To identify and fix bugs
- To plan new features

**How it's stored**:
- Anonymized and aggregated
- No personally identifiable information
- Cannot be traced back to specific images

### 4. Payment Information

**What we collect**:
- Purchase history (date, amount, credits purchased)
- Payment method type (card brand, last 4 digits)

**What we DON'T collect**:
- ❌ Full credit card numbers
- ❌ CVV codes
- ❌ Billing addresses (beyond what PayPro requires)

**How payments work**:
- All payment processing handled by **PayPro**
- We never see or store your full card details
- PayPro is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified
- [PayPro Privacy Policy](https://payproglobal.com/privacy-policy)

## How We Use Your Information

### 1. To Provide the Service

- **Process vectorization requests**: Send images to Vectorizer.ai API
- **Manage credits**: Track your balance and usage
- **Deliver results**: Send SVG back to you in Figma

### 2. To Communicate With You

- **Purchase confirmations**: Email receipts (via PayPro)
- **Credit expiration warnings**: Notify before credits expire
- **Service updates**: Important plugin changes (rare)
- **Customer support**: Respond to your inquiries

**We will NOT**:
- Send marketing emails without consent
- Share your email with third parties
- Spam you with promotions

### 3. To Improve Our Service

- **Fix bugs**: Identify and resolve technical issues
- **Optimize performance**: Analyze processing times and errors
- **Develop features**: Understand which presets/settings are used
- **Quality assurance**: Monitor conversion success rates

**Always anonymized**: Individual user data is never examined
