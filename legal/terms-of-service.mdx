## 1. Service Description

### 1.1 What We Provide

**Vectorized (AI Vectorizer) - Image Tracer** is a Figma plugin that provides:

- **AI Vectorization**: Convert bitmap images (PNG, JPG) to scalable SVG using Vectorizer.ai API
- **Smart Presets**: Pre-configured settings for logos, illustrations, line art, and high-fidelity conversion
- **HD Preview**: 2-credit preview before committing to full conversion
- **Custom Controls**: Adjust quality, detail, colors, and other parameters
- **Credit System**: Pay-as-you-go pricing with no subscriptions

### 1.2 How It Works

1. You select a bitmap image in your Figma file
2. You choose settings or a preset
3. You purchase credits to convert the image
4. We send your image to Vectorizer.ai API for processing
5. We return the SVG to your Figma canvas
6. Your image is deleted from our servers within 60 seconds

<Warning>
  **Third-Party Dependency**: Our service relies on Vectorizer.ai's API. Service interruptions may occur if Vectorizer.ai experiences downtime.
</Warning>

### 1.3 Service Availability
